Dyna Moe was a member of the first generation of the improv & sketch comedy-focused Upright Citizens Brigade Theatre New York. Starting in 1998, she's been a performer, teacher, director as well as working as the de facto art director designing posters, flyers, advertisements and other promotions for approximately 200 shows in that same period.

She's best known online as an illustrator for AMC television drama Mad Men, illustrating the extremely viral Mad Men Yourself avatar-maker, writing and illustrating best-selling book Mad Men: The Illustrated World and illustrating and designing the production's annual calendar for the entire run of the show.

Design and illustration clients include the Producers Guild of America, the City of Long Beach, CA, Lionsgate Television, Comedy Central, Chronicle Books, Random House, Marc Jacobs, The Minneapolis Star Tribune, A Special Thing Records, countless podcasts and individuals.

She has written and produced multiple web series for defunct platforms Funny or Die Presents (HBO), SuperDeluxe (Cartoon Network) and Topic.com. Her illustrated humor book mocking the cool kids and their various subcultures, Hipster Animals: A Field Guide, came out in 2015 and is currently available in the clearance bin of many fine used bookstores.

Born in Washington, D.C., she has lived in New York, Los Angeles, Seattle and San Diego and plans to move again as soon as the opportunity arises.
